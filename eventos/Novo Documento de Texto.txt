Samsung announces, for the second year in a row, its sponsorship of Lollapalooza Brasil, 
one of the biggest music festivals in the world. The activations during the 2019 edition of 
the event are part of a 360 campaign designed by the company to launch the Galaxy S10 line in the country.
Among the attractions, there is a 36-meter Ferris wheel that will be available to festival goers, as well as
 several experiences involving the brand's new smartphones. 
In addition, Samsung also sponsors the broadcast of Lolla BR on the Multishow channel, live.
